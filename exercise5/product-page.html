Sneaker Purle Orange Dunk High Retro

The iconic '80s model, created for the dance floor but loved by streetwear culture, returns with eye-catching glossy overlays and the collegiate colors of the original model. The Nike Dunk High Retro features a classic basketball design that brings retro '80s style back into everyday life, while the padded high-profile collar offers a retro look and comfortable fit.
